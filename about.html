About Us

ETOZ

Background

Founded in 22 April 2024, ETOZ is a burgeoning educational and technological enterprise dedicated to revolutionizing learning experiences. Founded with a vision to enhance the quality of education, ETOZ initially focuses on providing comprehensive school materials while gradually expanding its offerings to encompass a diverse array of skills and motivational classes. Operating primarily in Medan, Indonesia, ETOZ employs both online and offline platforms to deliver its educational programs, leveraging cutting-edge technology to augment the learning process. With a commitment to fostering a culture of continuous improvement, ETOZ endeavors to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and habits necessary for success in an ever-evolving world.

Let's Create Better Future

Vision:

ETOZ envisions a future where every individual has access to high-quality education and the tools to realize their full potential. By harnessing the power of technology and innovation, we aspire to create a global community of empowered learners who are equipped to thrive in the modern era. Our ultimate vision is to contribute to the creation of a more equitable and prosperous world, where education serves as a catalyst for positive change and socioeconomic advancement.

Mission:

ETOZ is on a mission to democratize education and transform lives through a multifaceted approach that integrates cutting-edge technology with personalized learning experiences. We are dedicated in:

  • Providing comprehensive school materials and educational resources to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values essential for both academic success and holistic personal growth, fostering resilience, a growth mindset, and strong character.
  • Developing innovative software solutions and educational tools that enhance the learning process and cultivate positive study habits.
  • Expanding access to education by leveraging online platforms and delivering engaging, interactive learning experiences.
  • Collaborating with educators, schools, and communities to tailor our programs to the specific needs and challenges of each individual learner.
  • Monitoring and evaluating student performance not only by grades but also by the depth of understanding and mastery of subject matter, with a focus on fostering a lifelong love for learning.

Through these endeavors, ETOZ is committed to making a meaningful impact on the world by equipping individuals with the education and resources they need to thrive in an increasingly complex and interconnected global society.
